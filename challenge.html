The Challenge

Ohio24

Can you ride across Ohio in 24 hours or less? Yep, its that simple.

Finishing Criteria

To complete the challenge and become a "Finisher" you must;

  1. Travel from the start point to the end point of the route
    1. Pass through designated control points and generally adhere to the "official" route unless safety requires otherwise.
    2. Travel at least 500 kilometers
  2. Complete the route (as specified above) in the span of a continuous 24 hour period.
  3. Use a human powered vehicle that is legal and safe to use on multi-use paths.
  4. Have a recorded GNSS (GPS) activity log that provides digital proof of passage. See previous efforts over on the Finisher page.
